Course management

Easily manage courses our classes online, setup from scratch takes just 10 minutes. The Floviz layout makes complex hierarchical organisation easy! One click branch addition / substraction easily structures your content.

  • Simple one click invites for course registration
  • Floviz dynamic layout

Share files

Files can be uploaded with just a drag and drop. Unisphere automatically reconstructs the folder(s) layout. Once uploaded you can move your files between nodes seemlessly.

  • Simple drag and drop file upload
  • Automated structure reconstruction from submitted folders
  • Add, subtract or move your files between nodes

Hand-in assignments

Create and receive assignements seemlessly within Unisphere. Unisphere allows you to bulk download all open assignments. Once submitted students can receive feedback and grading on completed assignments.

  • One click bulk assignments download
  • Grading and feedbacks for submitted assignments
